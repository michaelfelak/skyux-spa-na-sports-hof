Induction Guidelines


Purpose: To highlight the sports tradition of the community by recognizing and honoring those individuals and teams that have been instrumental in the creation, enlargement and maintenance of such tradition.
ELIGIBILITY/CRITERIA FOR CONSIDERATION
  • Any athlete, administrator, coach, official, supporter, team or organization that has made a major contribution to the community's sports tradition.
  • The nominee by his/her/its' involvement with athletics must have brought fame and glory to the tradition or had major involvement in improving the athletic opportunities and/or facilities of the community.
  • In order to be considered, a nominee must be a present or former resident of the North Augusta community
  • In order to be considered, a nominee must have been inactive in his/her athletic field of endeavor a minimum of five (5) years or if active, at the time of consideration, having contributed to the community in his/her field a minimum of twenty-five (25) years.
  • To be considered, a nominee must have demonstrated the highest standards of personal character and morals during the time of his/her contributions and such standards must still be present at the time of selection and induction.
